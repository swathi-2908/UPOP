Class Assignment

Students are split by pillar first — same-pillar students go into the same class where possible. Remainder students (when pillars don't divide evenly) are spread across classes.

ℹ
Split strategy: Students are grouped by session (date + venue), then split pillar-first — same-pillar students are kept together where possible. When a pillar is too large for one class, it's split across classes. Leftover students (remainders) are spread to the least-full classes.
You control: number of classes and max students per class. The preview updates live as you adjust.
